<b>MBCompass</b> is a free and open-source compass and navigation app
without ads, in-app purchases, or tracking. Lightweight, focused,
and built for real use.

<b>Compass</b>
- Accurate compass with true north and magnetic north
- Sensor fusion (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer) for stable readings
- Real-time magnetic field strength (µT)
- Auto-alert when accuracy drops low enough to matter

<b>GPS & Maps</b>
- Live location on OpenStreetMap
- Offline maps via Mapsforge point MBCompass to a folder
  containing any .map file and toggle between online and offline
- Toggle between OSM tiles and offline maps in Settings

<b>Track Recording</b>
- Record routes with start, pause, resume, and background support
- Smooth polyline rendering on the map
- Track stats: distance, duration, average speed, elevation gain/loss,
  min/max altitude, elevation profile
- Star and sort saved tracks
- Export as GPX : compatible with OsmAnd, Garmin, and any
  standard mapping tool

<b>App</b>
- Light, Dark, and AMOLED themes
- Custom bottom navigation bar
- Landscape orientation supported
- Screen stays on during navigation
- Runs on Android 6.0+
- Under 2 MB including offline map support

<b>Privacy</b>
- No analytics, no crash reporter, no ad network
- No Google Play Services dependency
- Location processed on-device only
- Source: https://github.com/CompassMB/MBCompass

<b>Not just a compass. Not a full navigation app.</b>
MBCompass sits in between, direction, live map, track recording,
and offline maps without unnecessary complexity.
