- Added offline map support: load any Mapsforge .map file directly from a folder on your device
- Added a toggle in Settings to switch between online osm tiles
  and offline maps
- Added per-app language support for Android 13+ users can now change MBCompass's language independently of system settings
- Migrated map screens from legacy XML layouts to full Jetpack Compose, significant performance and rendering improvements
- Fixed GPS waypoint density: added minimum time and distance
  filters to prevent excessive points being recorded when the
  device location updates too frequently
